The Yourts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?

Our luxury yurst are permanent structures fout feet of the ground. Each yurt is fully enclosed with walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. Your luxury campin experience includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water.

What should I bring?

Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress to changing weather with light layers of clothing. It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
